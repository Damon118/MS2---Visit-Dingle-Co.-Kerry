Places to see

Killarney

Killarney is a bustling town on the shores of Lough Leane in southwest Kerry. The town boasts numerous historical attractions, such as Ross Castle, and is home to the world-famous Lakes of Killarney and Killarney National Park.

View on map
Dingle

Dingle is a small port town in southwest Kerry, known for its rugged scenery, trails and sandy beaches. Dingle is famed for its authentic pubs, welcoming people, spectacular coastline and is a popular destination on the Wild Atlantic Way.

View on map
Tralee

Tralee is a town in County Kerry, in southwestern Ireland. It’s most known for hosting the world-renowned the Rose of Tralee International Festival annually. The Kerry County Museum and the National Folk Theatre of Ireland are also home to Tralee.

View on map
Listowel

Listowel is a heritage market town in Kerry and home to Ireland's oldest literary festival. The Listowel Food Fair runs every November and promotes local artisan food products and attracts food lovers from around the globe.
